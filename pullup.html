Pull Workout Routine

This routine is designed to help improve your pull-up strength, targeting key muscle groups in the back and arms. Follow the exercises below for an effective workout.

Main Workout

Exercise Sets Reps Notes
Inverted Rows 3 8-10 Adjust the height of the bar or TRX for difficulty.
Assisted Pull-Ups 3 4-6 Use a machine or band for assistance.
Negative Pull-Ups 3 3-5 Lower yourself slowly (3-5 seconds).
Dumbbell Rows 3 8-10 per arm Start with light weights, increase gradually.
Lat Pulldowns 3 10-12 Focus on form over weight.

Warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterwards. Adjust weights and reps according to your fitness level.
