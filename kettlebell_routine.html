Kettlebell Weekly Routine

This program is designed to build squat strength by targeting key muscle groups and improving mobility and explosiveness using kettlebell exercises.

Day 1: Posterior Chain & Explosiveness

Exercise Sets Reps
Kettlebell Swing (Traditional) 4 15
Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift 3 8 per leg
Kettlebell Snatch 3 6 per side
Kettlebell Good Morning 3 10

Day 2: Mobility and Stability

Exercise Sets Reps
Goblet Squat 3 10-12
Kettlebell Turkish Get-Up 3 2 per side
Kettlebell Dead Stop Swing 4 12
Kettlebell Suitcase Deadlift 3 8 per side

Day 3: Explosiveness & Bottom-Position Strength

Exercise Sets Reps
Kettlebell Swing (Traditional) 4 12
Kettlebell Pin Squat 3 6
Pause Goblet Squat 3 6-8 (3-second pause)
Kettlebell Plank Drag 3 10 drags per side

Always warm up before starting and cool down after your workout. Adjust weights and reps based on your fitness level.
