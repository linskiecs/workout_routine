3-Day Bench Press Accessory Workout Routine

Day 1: Triceps and Chest Focus

Exercise Sets Reps
Dumbbell Close-Grip Press 4 6-8
Dumbbell Overhead Triceps Extension 3 8-10
Dumbbell Bench Press 4 8-10
Dumbbell Chest Flys 3 10-12
Kettlebell Swings 3 12-15
Kettlebell Farmers Walks 3 30-60 seconds

Day 2: Shoulders and Back Focus

Exercise Sets Reps
Kettlebell Overhead Press 4 6-8 (single-arm or double-arm)
Dumbbell Arnold Press 3 8-10
Dumbbell Rows 4 8-10 per arm
Dumbbell Reverse Flys 3 10-12
Kettlebell Swings 3 15
Dumbbell Planks 3 30-60 seconds

Day 3: Triceps, Chest, Shoulders, and Core Focus

Exercise Sets Reps
Dumbbell Close-Grip Press 4 6-8
Kettlebell Skull Crushers 3 8-10
Dumbbell Floor Press 4 8-10
Dumbbell Lateral Raises 3 10-12
Kettlebell Turkish Get-Up 3 3-5 per side
Kettlebell Farmers Walks 3 30-60 seconds

Always warm up before starting and cool down after your workout. Adjust weights and reps based on your fitness level.
